### Newline
Two spaces after end of text. eg:  
`Here is a new line after two spaces above`

### Insert link
"Hard bracket then soft bracket"  
`[<text to display>](<filepath>)`

### Insert image link
`![]('/filepath')`

### Bold
`**<text>**` - double asterisks

### Italicise
`*<text>*` - single asterisks
